Frequently asked questions

What kind of plant feed do you feed the plants? ▼

We used top grade 100% organic, vegan plant food.

What services do you offer at the houseplant hotel? ▼

We sort everything from monitoring the plants daily to ensure that they are in the right temperature, to treating them to a misting once a day.

Do you have different room types available? ▼

Yes. We have the kitchen room, for plants that need medium light. We have the orangery for plants that need lots of light. And we have the wardrobe room for plants that aren't that bothered about light.
